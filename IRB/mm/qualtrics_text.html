Introduction

Thank you for your interest in participating in this research project. The survey will consist of the following parts:
  1. A description of the research project and participation requirements.
  2. A consent page providing you information regarding your rights as a participant, the costs and benefits of participating, and contact information for the researchers and review board. Please read through the consent information before continueing.
  3. A facility to provide updated biographic information if you wish to do so.
  4. A facility to provide a profile photograph.

Welcome

As part of ongoing research into online communities, we are working with Hero X to run a field experiment investigating how the composition of the community impacts participation in the community.

Ultimately, we would like to feature you in an email newsletter as a Hero X participant. A profile photo and bio would be part of a panel of 3 such profiles in the email. An example with fictional members is shown below.

If you choose to participate, your profile will be evaluated by independent raters alongside other profiles along various dimensions. These evaluations are to ensure comparability of the selected profiles for research purposes. A subsample of profiles most suitable for the project will be used in the newsletter. Participating does not guarantee inclusion in the newsletter.

If you would like to participate, please continue with this survey.

Participation is voluntary

It is your choice whether or not to participate in this research. If you choose to participate, you may change your mind and leave the study at any time. Refusal to participate or stopping your participation will involve no penalty or loss of benefits to which you are otherwise entitled.

What is the purpose of this research?

As part of ongoing research into online communities, we are working with Hero X to run a field experiment investigating how the composition of the community impacts participation in the community across different types of people.

How long will I take part in this research?

We anticipate your involvement requiring less than 1 hour of your time.

What can I expect if I take part in this research?

You will need to provide a suitable photograph of yourself and short biography for use in a user profile.

Will I be compensated for participating in this research?

As consideration for your participation, we will send you a $10 Amazon gift card.

If I take part in this research, how will my privacy be protected? What happens to the information you collect?

The profile information you provide will be public, as it currently is on the Hero X website. The profile information will also be reviewed by independent raters alongside other profiles to assess their comparability. Your participation in the research itself will be confidential. Typically, only project researchers will have access to that information. It is possible the information could be reviewed by auditors checking to see that the research is done properly.
